Project: Catalyst Website Redesign

For the final project for my user experience design class, we were assigned to redesign The Catalyst's website. We worked as a team of 3 to find the issues with the current site and come up with a solution through research and testing. Our research consisted of interviews, competitive analysis and visiting venues. We decided to focus on a mobile web design since many users would browse the site from their phone instead of their laptop or desktop computer. Technologies used : Abobe photoshop, inDesign, xd, illustrator.
