Project: Stapley Hildebrand

I was given the opportunity to redesign the ShredSF website. Originally they went with custom websites built internally but had recently began to use squarespace since it was accessible to more people rather than relying on a developer to make every change necessary. This was especially true for the blog since it has frequent updates. The blog on the redesign pulls its information via JSON object from squarespace so they can still easily update it but also maintain a custom design.
