Hello there,

My name is Riley and I am a designer who can code. I also have experience working as a project manager and have completed the UC Berkeley Project Management program.

I spend a lot of my time building websites for people as well as making videos and branding. Sometimes I like learning new software or skills such as 3D modeling or spray painting.

Tell me more

Well, I graduated in 2018 with a Bachelors of Science in Communication Design and then that summer attended General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. I also was able to intern at two different companies where I gained valuable experience.

Now I am freelancing as a web developer, brand designer, and video editor. When I am not working on a freelance project, I am either learning something new or practicing an existing skill. Recently I completed the project management certificate program at UC Berkeley.

For fun I like making custom art for people, playing video games, hanging out with friends, and traveling.
